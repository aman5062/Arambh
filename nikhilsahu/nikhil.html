About My School

LOYOLA HIGH SCHOOL KHUNTI was established in 1982 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. It is located in Urban area. It is located in KHUNTI block of KHUNTI district of Jharkhand. The school consists of Grades from 7 to 10. The school is Boys and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Hindi is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
